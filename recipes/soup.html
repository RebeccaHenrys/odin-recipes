Butternut Squash Soup

Description

A tasty soup that will warm you up on a cold autumnal evening

It's warming, it's spicy, and it's fun

Ingredients

  • Butternut Squahs
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Chilli flakes
  • Sage

Directions

  1. Roast your butternut squash in the oven for 45 minutes on 180 degrees.
  2. Chop your onion and garlic
  3. Sautee your onion and garlic until fragrant
  4. Add your butter squash to your pan and blend everything together
  5. Add sage and chilli flakes

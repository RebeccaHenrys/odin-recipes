Gnocchi

Description

A very tasty recipe that will keep everyone at the dinner table satisfied

It's a quick weeknight recipe that takes less than 30 minutes.

Ingedients

  • Gnocchi
  • Lemon
  • Asparagus
  • Walnuts

Steps

  1. Chop your asparagus and walnuts
  2. Fry your gnocchi on a medium heat until it begins to brown
  3. Add the asparagus and chopped walnuts
  4. Add in a squeeze of lemon juice
